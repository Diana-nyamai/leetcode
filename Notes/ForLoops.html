For loops notes

The importance of for loops is to help you avoid wasting time typing or to performs actions that are beyond human capability.

For example: Imagine where you had to manually type a million numbers to be displayed on the screen.it is practicay impossible and you would spend an eternity to just write down numbers

With for loops you can do that with just a single line of code; How cool is that?

how for loop works

for loops accepts three parameters

The first parameter is the initializer meaning the starting point

The second parameter is the condition where you want your loop to end at "the endpoint"

The third parameter is the the incrementer or decrementer. This basically specifies whether you want the loop to keep going forward or backwards

remember to separate the loop with a semi-colon

This is how a for loop looks like

for(var i=0; i<=10; i++)

the i can be anything; potato, mango anything you like to call your variable
